# Privacy Policy for Scholar Notes

**Last updated:** January 2026

Scholar Notes respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
Scholar Notes does **not collect**, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage
All notes created using Scholar Notes are stored **locally** in your browser using Chrome’s built-in storage (`chrome.storage.sync` or `chrome.storage.local`).

No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## Permissions
Scholar Notes uses only the permissions strictly necessary for its functionality:

- **Storage**: Used to save notes associated with academic papers.

## Third-Party Services
Scholar Notes does not use analytics, tracking tools, advertising services, or any third-party APIs.

## Data Sharing
No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any external entity.

## Changes
This privacy policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in this document.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the project’s GitHub repository.
